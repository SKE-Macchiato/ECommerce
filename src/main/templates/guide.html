Guide: How to buy our products


To order from Our website :

  1. Visit the product page for an item you wish to buy at product button on right side after Home button.
  2. Tap Buy to add to card or tab details for more information.
  3. When you've finished adding items to your cart
    • you can remove product here by tab x red button
  4. Check total money and then tap the checkout bar.
  5. Tab Confirm order.
  6. You can choose to pay with credit card or pay later.
